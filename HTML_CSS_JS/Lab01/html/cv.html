Awards

Awards and titles, in my opinion, are some of the most misleading forms of exposures to the outer world.
Most of the time they're not more than facades. Neither of those 2 things convey the characteristics, values or principles of the person holding the accolade. Rarely you're able to know who selected the person, or why said person was awarded. Instead, it's the instituion that gives value to both of these things. Yet still, despite the fact of having no clue about the organizations, we use accolades given by such organizations as a benchmark to measure people. That's why I put awards on the bottom of the page, if someone was even wondering that.
